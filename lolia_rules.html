Lolia Eco-Village Rules and Guidelines

FRAGRANCE-FREE ENVIRONMENT

Lolia is a fragrance free Lolia premises out for consideration of those who are chemically sensitive to synthetic fragrances and other scented products. Guests are asked to refrain from wearing perfume, aftershave, scented hand lotion, fragranced hair products, or using other artificially scented products excepting roll on deodorant/antiperspirant products.


ALCOHOL, TOBACCO AND ILLEGAL DRUGS

Lolia LLC is An illegal drug and alcohol free facility; this includes the exclusion of recreational and medically prescribed cannabis. There is a zero tolerance policy. The use of or being under the influence of illegal drugs and/or alcohol is inconsistent with the behavior expected of residents and program participants. The use, possession, distribution, or sale of illegal drugs or alcohol will not be tolerated on the premises. Lolia reserves the right to immediately expel anyone in violation of this policy without refund.

Smoking, including the use of electronic smoking devices, is permitted only in designated smoking area.


CHECK IN/OUT

Guest check-in begins at 3:00 PM on the day of arrival, and check-out on departure day is 11:00 AM. All guests must present photo ID and credit card at check-in. Early check-in after 9 AM on arrival day is possible for $25/person, subject to availability. Early check-in includes lunch.

All rooms must be vacated and keys left in room by 11:00 AM. A $25 per key replacement fee will be billed to the event organizer if any room keys are not returned at checkout.

Late checkouts are subject to availability with a minimum of 24 hours notice. Late checkouts may not exceed 4 hours. Fees may apply.
