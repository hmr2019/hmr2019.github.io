Lolia Eco-Village

The Lolia Community is an off-grid, ecologically sustainable, permaculture destination in the Puna district on the eastern side of the Big Island of Hawaii. The site is solar powered, and provides 100% of it's water from the rain which is stored in a state of the art water catchment system. The grounds have many foods growing from the bountiful papaya, macadamia nut, avocado, and mango trees, to pineapples, beans, taro, kale, kabocha squash, and more. It is a perfect site for retreats centered around conscious culture, meditation, yoga, dance, and sustainability.
